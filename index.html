About me

Hi, I'm Jacob! I'm a UX designer based in Northern Ireland with a mission to make design more accessible for all. I am very familiar with the UX design process from defining an idea/problem to solve, conducting research, creating prototypes and gathering feedback from iterative testing. My favorite aspect is accessibility as I believe it can be frequently overlooked in the design process, and I want to change that. That is my mission!

Accessibility Inclusive design User research Usability testing Rapid prototyping Interaction design

Outside the world of UX and 'vibecoding' in Cursor, I am a big believer in the power of a good reset. For me, that looks like capturing Northern Ireland through photography, getting lost in a Vacations record, or most importantly spending quality time with my dog, Benji. I find that the best design ideas usually appear to me when I'm furthest from the screen!
